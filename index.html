Our Story

How We Met

We met at a coffee shop in downtown Seattle on a rainy afternoon. Sarah was reading her favorite novel, and Michael accidentally spilled coffee near her table. What started as an apologetic conversation turned into hours of talking about life, dreams, and everything in between.

Three years later, on a beautiful sunset at the same coffee shop, Michael proposed with a vintage diamond ring. Today, we're thrilled to celebrate our love with all of you!
